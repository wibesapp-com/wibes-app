Why ?
Our purpose to start this company as a start-up roots itself in actually solving the problems we faced ourselves in shaping our own Start-ups and other Businesses.

We are consciously discriminating start-up Businesses then and today is to make it evidently clear about : How our Society today, which is now slowly emerging from the hangover of British Rule in India have absorbed a protective mindset to not be bold enough to have any Disruptive innovation nor have the courage to take risks.

Its been approx. 3 generations after independence having the necessary understanding and support system our young Indians today with the conviction to venture out on their own and who have the courage to enter an environment of risk are for sure rapidly emerging and if we work together, that would be a Wonderful opportunity for an Inclusive Business and Upliftment of Our Mother India.
